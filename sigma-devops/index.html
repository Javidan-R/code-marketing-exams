Our DevOps Consulting Process

DevOps

Explore

Infrastructure lays the foundation for business operations and must be stable, reliable and flexible enough to meet your current and at least medium-term needs.
At this stage, we:
  • Thoroughly analyze your business needs
  • Investigate existing infrastructure (if any)
  • Define & plan actions necessary to achieve desired results

Implement

From code to cloud, we automate every part of the DevOps process with CI/CD, organize container infra, and find optimal ways to make deployment faster & safer.
At this stage, we:
  • Develop new infrastructure & implement agreed changes
  • Focus on iterative approach with regular predictable deliveries
  • Measure each iteration results & suggest possible improvements

Maintain

Once the product goes live, it's time to take advantage of your market position through further optimization to maximize your ROI and productivity.
At this stage, we:
  • Identify and promptly eliminate possible bottlenecks
  • Monitor and analyze major KPIs (availability, performance, cost, etc.)
  • Experiment with new functionality & further improvements

Config management

In software development, configuration management refers to the items that need to be configured and managed for successful execution of a project. Our DevOps experts and Software architects work hand-in-hand to identify, control, and audit the development processes.

We do:
  • Reduce Mean Time to Recover (MTTR) from failure
  • Use Configuration-as-a-Code approach
  • Unify application configuration across environments
  • Perform configuration audits

Delivery Pipeline & CI

CI and CD are a solid foundation for the software development process. Together with version control and delivery pipelines, they enable a constant flow of changes to production through the automated software production line, providing better quality.

We do:
  • Build and re-architecture CI/CD pipelines, establishing environments for application
  • Implement independent deployment for multiple environments
  • Automate workflows with Teamcity, GitHub, and Jenkins

Automation & control

Automation & orchestration are both essential for successful digital transformation.  Using Ansible, Kubernetes, IaC approaches our DevOps experts will unify an approach to infrastructure management, making the system more predictable and reliable.

We do:
  • Automate repetitive tasks
  • Apply IaC and CAC approaches
  • Introduce centralization and orchestration
  • Manage multi-cloud infrastructure

Monitoring & alerting

Once your product hits the market, you will need to update it regularly and support your end-users. Our support & maintenance specialists can help you define a product maintenance strategy that aligns with your business goals and end-user expectations.

We do:
  • 1st, 2nd, and 3rd line support
  • Databases, Network, and Applications Monitoring
  • Maintenance and Proactive Event Management
  • User onboarding support through User Training and Product Manuals creation
